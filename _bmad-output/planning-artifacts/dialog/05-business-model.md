## Decision Structure

- **The "Buyer" (PI/Principal Investigator)**: Does not choose the tool, but approves the methodology and results in publications. Needs to trust the "standard" nature of the tool.
- **The "Champion" (Post-Doc/Lead Researcher)**: Often the one who recommends new frameworks to the lab.
- **The "Resource Holder" (Department)**: Controls GPU clusters. Will be the "Buyer" in Phase 2 (Managed IaaS).

## Strategic Objective

Target **3 to 5 specific labs** by solving a publicly identified limitation in their current pipeline. Use this as the "Recruitment Signal" to demonstrate intellectual leadership.
