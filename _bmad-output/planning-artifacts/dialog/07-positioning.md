# Positioning: Worldchain (PhysLink)

**Step:** Step 3 - Define Positioning
**Completed:** 2026-05-20

---

## Positioning Components

| Component | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Target Customer** | PhD students and post-docs in AI/Robotics at mid-sized, ambitious labs (ETH, MIT CSAIL, INRIA, TU Munich). |
| **Need / Opportunity** | Breaking the "6-month engineering cycle" required to build custom infrastructure before conducting research; solving the reproducibility crisis in World Model research. |
| **Category** | Standardized Adaptation & Infrastructure Toolkit for World Models (The "Glue Layer"). |
| **Key Benefit** | Drastic reduction in engineering overhead (months to days); results that are comparable, shareable, and reproducible across labs. |
| **Alternatives** | PyTorch "from scratch" (opaque), outdated/coupled GitHub forks, or RL-centric frameworks (LlamaIndex, RLlib) that ignore latent spaces. |
| **Differentiator** | Publicly accessible, standardized abstractions (Observation, Latent, Action) and exchange formats that replicate the "internal secrets" of top-tier labs (DeepMind, FAIR). |

---

## Positioning Statement

> **Pour les chercheurs et doctorants en Embodied AI qui passent plus de temps à coder leur infrastructure qu'à faire de la science, [PhysLink] est le toolkit d'adaptation standardisé qui transforme les World Models en briques interchangeables. Contrairement aux approches artisanales actuelles ou aux architectures verrouillées des géants de l'IA, notre outil apporte des abstractions communes (Observation, Latent, Action) et un format d'échange universel, permettant de fine-tuner un modèle pré-entraîné sur n'importe quel domaine en quelques jours. Nous ne construisons pas un modèle, nous construisons le langage commun qui permettra à la communauté de collaborer enfin à l'échelle.**

---

## Strategic Rationale

- **Trojan Horse Strategy**: By capturing the "mid-sized" academic labs, we influence the next generation of researchers who will bring the tool to major labs (DeepMind/AMI).
- **Standardization as Power**: In an ecosystem where every lab is an island, the one who provides the bridge (the glue layer) becomes the de facto standard.
- **Transparency vs. Gatekeeping**: Differentiating by providing the engineering "secrets" that major labs use as competitive moats.
