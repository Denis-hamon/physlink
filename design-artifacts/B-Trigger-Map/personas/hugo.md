# Persona: Hugo (The Overwhelmed PhD)

**Role:** 2nd/3rd Year PhD Student in ML/Robotics (ETH Zurich)
**Mantra:** "I need this to work this afternoon, and I need to be able to explain WHY it works."

## Context
Hugo is under constant pressure to publish SOTA results. He spends 80% of his time on "tensor plumbing" rather than algorithmic innovation.

## Driving Forces
- **Positive**: Wants to cite a standard that reviewers respect; wants the emotional high of a 90-minute result.
- **Negative**: Terrified of "magic" code that he can't debug or defend to his PI.

## Design Requirement
**Pipeline Debug Hooks & .explain() methods.** Every layer of PhysLink must be transparent and inspectable.
