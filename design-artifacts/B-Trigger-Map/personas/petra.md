# Persona: Petra (The Prolific Post-doc)

**Role:** Post-doctoral Researcher / Team Lead
**Mantra:** "I'm building my career on this lab's reproducibility standards."

## Context
Petra supervises multiple students. She is frustrated when she cannot compare results between students because their internal implementations differ subtly.

## Driving Forces
- **Positive**: Wants to list "introduced standardized research pipelines" on her Professorship application.
- **Negative**: Fears the political cost of recommending a tool that becomes "abandonware" in 12 months.

## Design Requirement
**Trust Signals & Standardized Buffers.** Needs a rigorous CHANGELOG, clear licensing, and data formats that enable internal comparison.
